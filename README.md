# Consumer Spending & Grocery Inflation Analytics

This project analyzes household spending patterns across different retail formats (Local, Supermarket, and Quick Commerce) to track inflation and store loyalty.

## 🛒 Performance Overview
* **Highest Earner:** Dmart (32.27% share of spending)
* **Cheapest Store:** Instamart
* **Best in Quick Commerce:** Zepto
* **Primary Category:** Personal Care (65.6% of total spend)

## 📈 Analysis Features
* **Inflation Impact:** Tracks the gap between "Base Price" and "Avg Price Paid" (e.g., Basmati Rice showing a ~5% increase).
* **Convenience Premium:** Visualizes the price markup when buying staples like Wheat Flour on Quick Commerce vs. Local Kiranas.
* **Store Loyalty:** A Treemap analysis showing Dmart and Local Kirana as the most frequented hubs.

## 💡 Key Takeaway
The data reveals that while Supermarkets (Dmart) capture the most budget, Quick Commerce platforms (Zepto/Blinkit) are used strategically for specific high-frequency items despite the convenience markup.

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* **Concepts:** Market Basket Analysis, Inflation Tracking, Retail Analytics
